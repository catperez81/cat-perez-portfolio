Introduction

I lead product and design for HealthSherpa, a Healthcare Startup in San Francisco. I placed first in the Salesforce $1M hackathon a couple of years ago with some friends. Our entry was a simple and intuitive version of Healthcare.gov. After the win, I knew I had to pursue a career dedicated to improving the flawed and dated system that is healthcare. Outside of work, I enjoy good coffee, pizza anytime of day, and road trips with my wife and pup.

Education, communication, collaboration, and honesty are all things I find important in life and work. I've learned to value these since I've spent most of the past 2 years of my career on very small teams. It truly makes all the difference.

Where I'm From

I am from the East Coast - New Jersey to be exact (born and raised). My mother is from South Korea and my father is from Puerto Rico. I grew up around a lot of my father's family (most in NJ), so I picked up some Spanish along the way. I attended college in Long Island, NY - and soon after I graduated moved to San Francisco where I lived for the past 8 years. I've recently relocated to Los Angeles, where I currently live and work remotely.
